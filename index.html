ComicFest is July 12-15, 2018 at the Greater Ft. Lauderdale / Broward Convention Center

ComicFest is the LARGEST Comic Con in South Florida! ComicFest is 4 days of fun featuring celebrity guests, comic book creators, voice actors, industry guests, cosplayers, artists, writers, panels, Q&A’s, films & shorts, costume & cosplay contests, vendors, parties, anime, workshops, video gaming and more!

ComicFest is for fans of pop culture, superheroes, science fiction, anime, cartoons, video games, and more. Dress up in costume or come as you are for this family friendly event.

GREATER FT. LAUDERDALE / BROWARD CONVENTION CENTER LOCATION & PHONE NUMBER:
1950 Eisenhower Blvd, Fort Lauderdale, FL 33316 • (954) 765-5900
